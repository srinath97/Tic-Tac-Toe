PROBLEM: TIC-TAC-TOE is a two player game where the players are given alternate chances. Player 1 is denotedd by 'X' and player 2 is denoted by 'O'. If 'X' or 'O' 
comes continuosly horizontal or vertical or diagonal then that player won.

This program works in DEV-C++ version 5.8.3.
